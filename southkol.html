6 Ballygunge Place, Kolkata

In terms of buffet restaurants in Kolkata, 6 Ballygunge Place is a local favorite and is often mentioned in connection with the Ilish Festival. Chef Sushanta Sengupta creates a special menu of mouthwatering treats just for the celebration. The celebration wouldn't be complete without sampling the fishes that can be found at 6 Ballygunge Place, which perfectly matches the seasonal availability of fish. For fish enthusiasts in particular, the festival is not the only occasion to visit this eatery.

Address: Dr Amiya Bose Sarani Rd Near Patah Bhavan School, Kolkata (Calcutta) 700019 India

Hatari

A distinctive and luxurious dining experience is provided by the fine dining establishment Hatari. The freshest ingredients were used to produce a variety of meals from around the world. With a contemporary and chic decor, the ambiance is welcoming and pleasant. The staff offers outstanding service and is attentive and friendly. Hatari is the ideal location to savor a fantastic meal whether you're looking for a special occasion or a romantic dinner.
